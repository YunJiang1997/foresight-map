Data, Reporting & Analytics
From Data Assembly to Always-On Business Intelligence
FinanceHRCorporate Strategy
Provisional Use Case
Polaris (40+ ERPs), Workday People Analytics, and Kyriba create rich data assets that today feed largely manual reporting cycles. Layering AI/ML turns these into always-on intelligence: GenAI drafts board-ready group reporting narratives in minutes; ML detects anomalies across consolidated multi-entity data; People Analytics predicts attrition and surfaces pay equity gaps across 80+ nationalities; and corporate intelligence platforms synthesise competitor signals from patents, media, and startup databases in real time.

Process & Workflow Automation
Eliminating Manual Handoffs Across Shared Services
FinanceHRLegal
Provisional Use Case
Finance (AP/AR processing, bank statement downloads, reconciliation steps), HR (interview scheduling, onboarding sequences, payroll self-service), and Legal (contract routing, matter assignment) all face the same problem: high-volume, rule-based handoffs consuming professional time. RPA executes the steps; FinTech platforms automate the transactions; AI orchestrates the exception flows. Process mining (Celonis, flagged as the #1 LEAP bottleneck) identifies exactly where each workflow deviates from the global standard.

Document & Text Intelligence
Extracting Value from Unstructured Text Across the Business
FinanceLegalHR
Provisional Use Case
Invoice documents across 40+ heterogeneous ERPs, contracts in the Global Contracting Platform, regulatory publications across 70+ markets, and internal communications in 30+ languages all share the same bottleneck: reading, classifying, and drafting text at scale is time-consuming and error-prone. Governance, Risk & Compliance
Shifting from Periodic Sampling to Continuous Assurance
LegalFinance
Provisional Use Case
Internal audit currently samples; regulations change faster than manual monitoring can track; the EU AI Act now mandates logging every AI-assisted decision. A Continuous Assurance platform closes all three gaps: ML covers 100% of transactions rather than a sample, flagging control weaknesses and fraud patterns in real time; blockchain creates tamper-proof audit trails across affiliates; LegalTech governance tooling produces the EU AI Act traceability log as a structured asset rather than a compliance burden. Post-quantum cryptography protects sensitive legal communications long-term.

Planning, Budgeting & Forecasting
From Static Annual Plans to Dynamic Forward-Looking Intelligence
FinanceHRCorporate Strategy
Provisional Use Case
Treasury cash forecasting, workforce headcount planning, and strategic portfolio scenario planning are three distinct exercises today — each periodic, each largely manual. The shift is to a single continuous planning posture: ML generates rolling 13-week cash forecasts from Kyriba data; AI workforce platforms produce dynamic 12-18 month demand forecasts tied to business projections; simulation models stress-test strategic portfolio scenarios — tariff shocks, M&A impacts, category shifts — before capital is committed. Digital twins enable cost-of-production simulations at site level before process changes are approved.

Learning & Employee Experience
Immersive, Personalised Learning at Global Scale
HR
Provisional Use Case
19,000 employees across 70+ countries need consistent brand immersion, safety training, and skills development — but in-person sessions are costly and geographically constrained. VR/XR on the Horizon platform delivers safety training with 75%+ information recall (vs. classroom), brand storytelling for new joiners, and remote expert guidance collapsing geography as a barrier. AI personalises learning paths by role and career aspiration and localises content instantly into all required languages. PR India's VR onboarding is already live across 14 locations — a deployable template.
